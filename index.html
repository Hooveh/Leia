"This will be fun to watch."

Hyper and lively, Leia is a brave soul with plenty of fire in it. As Leth's twin sister, her long, disheveled hair goes far past her shoulderblades, and when it comes time to fight, her eyes display a manic enthusiasm, yearning to unleash a raging inferno.

But most of the time, she's a rather lovable dolt, no less one than her brother.
